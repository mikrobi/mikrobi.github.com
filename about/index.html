About

Hi there! I’m Jakob, a software engineer living in Berlin, Germany. My main focus is in building backends, web clients and mobile apps.

I build great stuff for developers with other great people at GitHub.

Before that I maintained the backends of the mbrace and Roundhere apps and led their iOS mobile team. I used to work with several other business after I co-founded my first company with my fellow Marin Zec at the age of 18.

I enjoy teaching others and regularly talk about my experience in public.

You can find me on GitHub and Twitter.
